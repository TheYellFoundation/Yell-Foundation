Our Mission

Language learning should feel like an adventure.

Y.E.L.L. (Youth Education Linguistics Learning) creates a vibrant space where kids who are learning English — or for whom it isn't a first language — can build real confidence in communication.

We specialize in phonetics and reading support, turning tricky sounds into predictable patterns and turning "I can't say that" into "listen to me now."

"We make learning fun, build confidence, and help every student discover the joy and power of communication."

What We Offer

Our Programs

All classes are free, welcoming, and designed for kids of all backgrounds learning English.

🔤

Phonetics Foundations

We break down English sounds into clear, learnable patterns. Kids practice vowels, consonants, and blends through games and activities that make phonetics click.

All ages
📖

Reading & Fluency

From sounding out words to reading sentences with expression, this program builds the reading confidence that carries into every subject at school.

Elementary focus
🗣️

Conversation & Confidence

Speaking is the hardest part. We create low-pressure practice conversations so students get real reps in a kind, supportive environment.

All levels
Who We Are

Run by students, for students.

Y.E.L.L. is organized and taught entirely by high school students from Newton who are passionate about the English language and about making a real difference in their community.

We understand what it's like to be in a classroom where language feels like a wall instead of a door. That's exactly why we do this — to help younger kids climb over it and find their voice.
